What are undertones?

Your skin's undertone is the color underneath the surface. You can have the same skin color as someone, but a different undertone, which are broken down like this: Cool (pink, red or bluish undertones) Warm (yellow, peachy, golden undertones) Neutral (a mix of warm and cool undertones)

Classifications Undertones Example
Cool Pale or Rosy
Warm Yelllow, Peachy or Golden
Neutral Mix of Warm & Cool
